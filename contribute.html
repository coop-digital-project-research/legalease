2. What information does LGE obtain?

When you first set up your LG Smart TV, you will be prompted to enter certain information, such as the country where the LG Smart TV is being set up and your zip code or postcode. This is to enable us to configure your LG Smart TV so that it displays the appropriate services and content for your region.

When your LG Smart TV is connected to the Internet, during the set-up process and thereafter, we will receive certain information, including Device Information, that is needed to authenticate and set up your LG Smart TV, as well as power its basic functions. This information is necessary for the basic functioning of the Smart TV and does not require agreement with this Privacy Policy. However, in order to gain access to the full range of Smart TV services, you must agree to our Privacy Policy, which facilitates a greater exchange of information between your LG Smart TV and our systems.

The information that we may receive when you use your LG Smart TV falls into the following categories:

  • Device Information. This refers to information collected about your LG Smart TV and certain related devices, such as your set-top box, remote control, and DVD or Blu-ray player. Device Information may include information to authenticate your LG Smart TV with our servers, the country code and zip code or postcode of the region where your LG Smart TV is being set up, your IP Address, device type (e.g., set-top box, DVD player, etc.), MAC address (or in some circumstances, a truncated MAC address), software platform version, language, set-top box manufacturer, screen resolution, and model number.
  • Viewing Information. This refers to information about your interactions with program content, including live TV content, movies, and video on demand. Viewing Information may include the name of the channel or program watched, requests to view content, details of actions taken while viewing (e.g., play, stop, pause, etc.), the duration that content was watched, and input method (RF, Component, HDMI).
  • Basic Usage Information. This refers to information which records your interactions within the LG Smart TV, such as the menu items you click on, the apps you access (but not your activity within apps), what channels are available to you, and information regarding external devices connected to the Smart TV.
  • Voice Information. This refers to voice commands and associated data (such as information about the input device that records your voice) used to recognize and act upon the command, OS information, TV model information, content provider, channel information and service results.

As well as receiving the above information directly from your LG Smart TV, LGE may receive further information that you choose to provide in order to access certain LG Smart TV Services. For example, some of our services require that you become a member of LG Content Store, which may be subject to separate terms. You may join LG Content Store either through your LG Smart TV or by other means, such as through certain LG websites. This Membership Information may include your user ID, password, telephone number, name, date of birth, gender, email address, address, social networking service ID, security question answers, purchase history, and related payment information, such as credit card information or details of your PayPal account and more.

Device Information, Viewing Information, Basic Usage Information and Voice Information are generally handled by LGE in such a way that members of your household are not identified or identifiable from that information, so such information is not “personal information.” If you become a member of LG Content Store and are logged-in to LG Content Store when you use LG Smart TV Services, however, then certain Device Information, Basic Usage Information, Viewing Information, and Voice Information may be associated with your Membership Information in such a way that it may become personally identifiable to you.

3. How will LGE use the information it obtains?

We use information obtained from your LG Smart TV (both non-personal information and, where applicable, personal information) for various purposes, including:

  • to provide our Smart TV services;
  • to analyze, review and improve the products and services we offer;
  • to understand how customers are using LG Smart TVs;
  • to develop new products and services;
  • to provide you with a user-friendly navigation experience;
  • to protect and defend our rights and property, including by way of legal proceedings;
  • to collect payment for content you purchase;
  • for marketing;
  • for internal record keeping;
  • to deliver advertisements.

We may combine information obtained from your LG Smart TV Services, including personal information, with information that you provide to us by other means, for the purposes consistent with this Privacy Policy. We also may receive your information from third-party social networking services when you choose to connect to our Services with those social networking services’ accounts.

Depending on the country where you are based and the model of LG Smart TV that you own, LG Smart TV Services may include the following features; however, some of the features may have different names and may be combined with other features:

Live Plus

Live Plus recognizes the content you are watching and provides interactive services based on the content you’re watching. We may also use information collected via Live Plus to improve our products and your experience, and this information may be shared with third parties.

SmartShare

SmartShare enables you to play media content from storage devices connected to your Smart TV.

Search

You can search for content on your LG Smart TV. Information about the search queries you enter may be used to improve our search-recommendation services and search results.

In addition, some LG Smart TV Services require you to become a member of LG Content Store. These include certain social networking functionality that enables you to access and use sites such as Facebook and Twitter through your LG Smart TV.

LGE reserves the right to add or remove services from its Smart TV services and to modify its Smart TV services from time to time.

How we use Device and Basic Usage Information

We use the Device Information and Basic Usage Information collected to provide you with LG Smart TV Services. We also use such information to improve and develop our LG Smart TV products and services from time to time.

How we use Viewing Information

We may collect and use your Viewing Information to provide our Live Plus service as described above. Depending on which Smart TV service you are using, we may use your Viewing Information to provide you with recommendations of TV content that may be of interest to you, to contribute to aggregate statistics to determine the popularity of particular TV content, to serve you with advertisements for content, products, and services that may be of interest to you, to analyze how our services are being used, and make improvements to them.

Apart from this Privacy Policy, you will be provided a specific opportunity to agree or disagree with the collection and use of Viewing Information. Please note, however, that anytime that you request to view content through a video on-demand service provided by LG, such as, your LG Smart TV will transmit information to enable video playback to LG. This information will only be used to provide the content you request, unless you have agreed to the Viewing Information agreement (in which case it may be used for the additional purposes described in that agreement).

You may prevent the collection and use of your Viewing Information in connection with the Live Plus service by clicking the appropriate on-screen button in the Settings menu. Please note that if you do so, your Viewing Information will continue to be used for other LG Smart TV services.

How we use your Voice Information

Many LG Smart TVs come with a remote control or other input devices that can be operated using voice commands. We may use your Voice Information to power the voice activation used by the Smart TV or input device (e.g., remote control). If you do not agree to our use of your Voice Information then you will not be able to use the voice command and recognition features. Apart from this Privacy Policy, you will be provided a specific opportunity to agree or disagree with the collection and use of Voice Information. Please be aware that if your spoken word includes personal or other sensitive information, such information will be among the Voice Information captured through your use of voice-recognition features.

How we use your Membership Information

Your Membership Information may be used in order to provide you with LG Smart TV Services that require membership of LG Content Store. Your Membership Information may also be combined with Device Information, Viewing Information, Basic Usage Information, and/or Voice Information. Our use of Membership Information is more fully described in the separate terms that govern membership of LG Content Store.

Personalized Advertising

We may display relevant personalized advertising on your LG Smart TV. Such advertising may be based on your Viewing Information (where we have your agreement to use Viewing Information), Membership Information, Basic Usage Information, your use of third-party applications, advertisements that have previously been clicked on, and other factors intended to select advertisements that may be of interest. When displaying advertisements, we may work with advertising companies and other business partners, who may receive your Viewing Information and may share with us information relating to the advertisements that they display on your LG Smart TV including, for example, the dates and times of advertisements shown, details of the advertisements shown, and your interactions with such advertisements.

Apart from this Privacy Policy, you can to choose at any time whether you want to receive personalized advertising by agreeing or disagreeing with the separate document entitled “Personalized Advertising.”

If you have chosen not to receive personalized advertising, your LG Smart TV may still display advertisements in connection with the provision of certain Smart TV services. Those advertisements would be selected using non-personal information such as the region where your LG Smart TV is located, as indicated by your TV’s Device Information, and the content that your LG Smart TV is displaying. In addition, if the Live Plus service is on, you may see interactive overlays that invite you to purchase products or services in connection with the content you are watching.
